LLG Signature Teams

SIGNATURE PROGRAM

Lady Lightning Gold is our National Teams in the Lady Lightning Fastpitch Organization. LLG is made up of players with the passion, desire and skills to play D1 softball. Our teams travel to National Championship events and National Showcase events to gain both experience and exposure against the best competition in the country.

LLG players are working toward an opportunity to become a student athlete at both the Major D1 programs and Mid-major D1 programs. Our players perform both in the classroom and on the field, as they prepare for life as a collegiate player.

LLG National Teams cover age groups: 12U(Uprising),14U (Futures), 16U (Elite), 16U (Midwest), 18U (Prospects), 18U (Premier) and our Flagship Sponsored Team (LLG DEMARINI PLATINUM SQUAD)
